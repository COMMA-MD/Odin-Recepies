How to make a cocktail

Ingredients

  • 1 Granny Smith apple
  • 2 ounce gin or vodka
  • 1 ounce Cointreau or St. Germain liquor
  • 1 Lime

How to prepare

  1. Slice apple in half and cut a slice out for garnish, and set aside with some lime juice on to prevent browning.
  2. Shred, or finely dice the rest of the apple.
  3. Place apple into cocktail shaker and muddle to mash the apple.Add the lime juice and gin, and muddle some more
  4. Add the gin or vodka and liquor, and ice and shake vigorously.
  5. Double strain into a martini glass, and garnish with apple slice.
  6. Enjoy the drink
